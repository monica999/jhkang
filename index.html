    About

    I'd like to experience more, learn more and be happier

    • 2000-2007

      Learn

      This time was full of curiosity and enthusiasm; and it drove me take a double major in Architecture and Visual Art.
      During I took three years off from school, I met various kinds of people in different fields whom I'd never met in my life.
      As a result, I learned various life lessons and it made me to widen my vision.

    • 2007-2010

      Be deep, be wide

      Working in one of the major mobile agencies in Korea, that works closely with Samsung and LG opened my eyes to see the future of the mobile market and taught me professionalism.
      Soon I joined another design company.
      There, I had a chance to learn interior design, prints, packaging designs and web design from scratch.
      It'ls my one of the happiest memories of my working life because I was full of passion and excitement to learn new things with good colleagues.

    • 2010-Present

      Multinational

      Working with 9 Asian countries in real-time in Asian country such like South Korea, Singapore and Taiwan gave me many insights (not only design, but also about the importance of having an international attitude), friends and chances to know other cultures.
      My challenges have made me improve my skills, made me a better designer and made me a better person day by day.

    • To be
      a happy
      designer!
